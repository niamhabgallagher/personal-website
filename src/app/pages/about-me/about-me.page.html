About Me

Background.

I am currently an undergraduate student studying for a degree in Computer Science and Software Development at the University of Liverpool. I have an interest in Development and I have completed a placement in the industry.

Interests.

In my spare time recently I have been teaching myself some new skills. I have been trying to learn my native language Irish as it would be an honour to be able to speak my mother tongue. Before corona virus I was attending classes at the Liverpool Irish Centre but currently I am learning through Duolingo and a text book. I have also decided to pick up knitting and have been trying to knit myself a blanket. I enjoyed knitting with my Granny when I was younger so it brings back fond memories.

I have also been getting into my creative side by taking up drawing sketches to raise money for the Black Lives Matter movement where I have raised almost £100 for some charities, including The Bail Project.
